[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - H&H Tools[/h1]
[i]"The right tool for the end of the world."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Regional faction system:[/b] adds civil settlements, rough settlements, raider gangs, and tribes for California, Arizona, Cascadia, and Nuevo Texico, plus ghoul ghost towns, super-mutant strongholds, mutant armies, S'Lanter tribes, Numen tribes, and other mutant communities.
[*] [b]Faction populations:[/b] supplies dedicated settler, guard, trader, mayor, child, miner, hunter, logger, farmer, raider, and tribal pawn kinds, with regional apparel and weapon pools.
[*] [b]Cultures and names:[/b] adds American, Bear, Bull, Cascadian, Nuevo Texican, raider, ghoul, mutant, Numen, and S'Lanter cultures; regional pawn names; faction-name makers; settlement names; religion names; and a large library of Fallout-style world-feature and landmark names.
[*] [b]Starting-scenario retheme:[/b] rewrites Crashlanded as an airship wreck, Rich Explorer as a vault dweller leaving safety behind, Lost Tribe as survivors of a machine attack, and Naked Brutality as a wastelander robbed and left for dead.
[*] [b]FCP integration:[/b] adds shared ballistic, energy, melee, and unarmed weapon tags and assigns compatible West Tek xenotypes to Brotherhood, Enclave, Great Khans, Legion, NCR, PRC, Raider, United States, and Wastelander factions when those mods are active.
[*] [b]Vanilla/DLC faction conversion:[/b] patches player factions, ancients, refugees, Empire, sanguophages, pilgrims, cultists, insects, salvagers, and traders into the shared FIP faction framework.
[*] [b]Visuals:[/b] includes custom icons for democratic, despotic, tribal, raider, mutant, mechanist, remnant, American, and Whitespring identities. It does not replace pawn bodies or equipment textures.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] H&H Tools is both a shared framework and a visible faction pack. Adding its factions to an existing world cannot rebuild that world's original faction distribution; a new game is recommended.
[*] Removing it from a save that uses dependent FIP factions, cultures, pawn kinds, or names is not supported.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
