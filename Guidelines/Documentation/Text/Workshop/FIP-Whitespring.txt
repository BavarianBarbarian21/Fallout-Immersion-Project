[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Whitespring[/h1]
[i]"Courtesy, control, and comfort for the chosen few."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] Reimagines [b]Royalty[/b]'s Empire as the [b]Whitespring[/b] hierarchy for a Fallout playthrough.
[*] Reworks titles, honor, transport, apparel, and ceremonial language into Fallout-appropriate ranks and terminology.
[*] Adds compatibility support for [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Empire[/b], [b]Deserters[/b], and [b]FCP Enclave[/b] apparel crossover content.
[*] Replaces untouched imperial flavor with a cleaner Whitespring identity across localization and patch layers.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Royalty DLC[/b] - Required (core empire systems)
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] This module is in open beta. Balance passes, naming cleanup, and compatibility refinements are still ongoing, so targeted feedback is useful.
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a complete modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but it is designed to complement FCP and other Fallout-friendly RimWorld mods. You can also find the project in the FCP Discord's FIP channel: [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]Join here[/url]
[/list]
