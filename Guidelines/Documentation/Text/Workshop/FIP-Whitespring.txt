[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Whitespring[/h1]
[i]"The government survived. Whether that was good news is classified."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Enclave conversion:[/b] rewrites Royalty's Empire as the Whitespring Enclave, replacing imperial framing with a surviving pre-war government and military hierarchy.
[*] [b]Rank progression:[/b] renames royal titles and their supporting text to Enclave ranks while preserving Royalty's established permit, ceremony, and progression mechanics.
[*] [b]Political terminology:[/b] converts honor into [b]votes[/b] and updates related quests, rewards, favors, alerts, and interface text to match the new political framing.
[*] [b]Aerospace terminology:[/b] renames shuttles and shuttle-related content to [b]vertibirds[/b] and [b]XVB02[/b] craft where appropriate.
[*] [b]Compatibility rewrites:[/b] conditionally converts Vanilla Factions Expanded - Empire and Deserters material into Enclave and Brotherhood of Steel identities when those mods are loaded.
[*] [b]Apparel integration:[/b] aligns supported rank and faction apparel with the Enclave presentation without adding a separate equipment progression system.
[*] [b]Visual scope:[/b] this module currently ships no in-game texture replacement; its overhaul is delivered through labels, descriptions, faction text, titles, quests, and conditional patches.
[*] [b]Mechanics scope:[/b] the underlying Royalty mechanics remain intact - this is a setting conversion, not a new permit or title system.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Royalty DLC[/b] - Required for the Empire, titles, honor, permits, ceremonies, and shuttle content being converted.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Empire[/b] - Optional Enclave compatibility layer.
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Deserters[/b] - Optional Enclave/Brotherhood compatibility layer.
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3665532846][b]FIP - H&H Tools[/b][/url] - Recommended for the wider FIP faction framework.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Whitespring intentionally preserves Royalty's gameplay rules while changing their setting, faction identity, and language.
[*] Optional compatibility text is loaded only when the relevant Vanilla Expanded module is active.
[*] Existing saves may retain some generated names, quest text, or cached titles until new content is created.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
