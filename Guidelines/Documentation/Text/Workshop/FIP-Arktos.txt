[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Arktos[/h1]
[i]"Reclaim the wasteland, one biome at a time."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]New world generation:[/b] adds 23 Fallout-oriented biomes, including wasteland, ruin, crater, divide, malpais, Mojave, mesa, canyon, aqueduct, floodplains, central valley, brahmin woods, preserve, cranberry bog, redwood, sequoia, marina, creek, and several cold-region variants.
[*] [b]Custom biome logic:[/b] dedicated biome workers and an Arktos world-generation pass place regional environments according to temperature, elevation, rainfall, rivers, roads, and nearby urban terrain instead of simply relabeling vanilla biomes.
[*] [b]Landmarks and mutators:[/b] adds suburbs, stores, gas stations, construction sites, harbors, junkyards, uplink stations, stockpile bunkers, launch sites, garrisons, infested settlements, fire-ant nests, and ancient smoke, toxic, and heat vents.
[*] [b]New fire-ant ecosystem:[/b] adds a fire-ant faction, worker ants, soldiers, common ants, larvae, drones, queens, hives, and cocoons with custom bodies, life stages, combat roles, and complete ant artwork.
[*] [b]Renamed and retextured wildlife:[/b] converts vanilla insectoids into giant worker ants, giant ants, and giant soldier ants; adds ant visuals; and supplies optional custom axolotl fish art for Odyssey and Vanilla Fishing Expanded.
[*] [b]Punga conversion:[/b] renames Ambrosia and its high, tolerance, addiction, need, withdrawal, and thought text to [b]punga[/b] and [b]punga warmth[/b].
[*] [b]Ecosystem compatibility:[/b] redistributes plants and animals from FCP Plants, FCP Animals, Vanilla Plants Expanded families, Vanilla Animals Expanded families, and Vanilla Fishing Expanded; also integrates Ancient Urban Ruins and Vanilla Landmarks Expanded.
[*] [b]Visuals:[/b] includes custom biome icons, full ant textures and corpses, and axolotl variants. These are real replacements and additions, not merely unused texture files.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Arktos changes the generated planet. Start a new world to receive its full biome, road, feature, landmark, plant, and animal distribution.
[*] Existing saves cannot be retroactively rebuilt into the new world map.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
