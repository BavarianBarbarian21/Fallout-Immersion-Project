[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Sunset[/h1]
[i]"Old worlds fade. The wasteland keeps the useful parts."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Medieval 2 conversion:[/b] removes the three kingdoms and three clans from world generation, disables the New Kingdom scenario and two incompatible opportunity quests, and hides medieval structures, buildings, equipment, and the Norse structure meme where applicable.
[*] [b]Modernized trading:[/b] converts the Merchant Guild into wasteland caravan companies and replaces medieval trader stock and pawn groups with modern and Fallout-compatible goods.
[*] [b]Medieval terminology:[/b] alchemy becomes [b]extract brewing[/b] and grapes become [b]bloody vine[/b], with connected labels and descriptions updated for the setting.
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Settlers conversion:[/b] reworks faction identities, pawn pools, weapon tags, traders, and equipment access; adds bighorner caravan carriers; and disables the Wanted and Caravan Raid incidents plus the Bandits scenario.
[*] [b]Settler renames and cleanup:[/b] [b]chemshine[/b] becomes [b]rotgut[/b], [b]chemroot[/b] becomes [b]radseng[/b], and [b]Faro[/b] becomes [b]caravan table[/b]; the chemshined-muffalo behavior is removed.
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Tribals conversion:[/b] renames its technology eras to [b]Survivalist[/b], [b]Tribal[/b], [b]Prospector[/b], [b]Settler[/b], [b]Vault-Tec[/b], and [b]Pre-War[/b].
[*] [b]Visual replacements:[/b] includes Fallout-compatible textures for the archery target and training dummy retained from Medieval 2.
[*] [b]Mechanics scope:[/b] Sunset actively changes world generation, incidents, scenarios, pawn groups, trader inventories, tags, and compatibility behavior; it is not only a rename pack.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] No DLC or external mod is a hard requirement.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3665532846][b]FIP - H&H Tools[/b][/url] - Strongly recommended for the Fallout factions, cultures, pawn identities, and shared integration used by the conversions.
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Medieval 2, Settlers, and Tribals[/b] - Optional source mods; each patch activates independently when its target is loaded.
[*] [b]Ideology DLC[/b] - Optional; enables the conditional medieval structure-meme cleanup.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Sunset deliberately suppresses incompatible content from its supported source mods; review the feature list before adding it to a campaign built around those scenarios or factions.
[*] The three source-mod integrations are conditional and do not create hard dependencies.
[*] World factions and incidents already generated in a save may not be retroactively removed; a new world gives the cleanest conversion.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
