[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Corvega[/h1]
[i]"See the wasteland from behind a genuine pre-war steering wheel."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Fallout vehicle branding:[/b] converts supported Vanilla Vehicles Expanded cars into [b]Chryslus[/b] and [b]Corvega[/b] models while preserving their original vehicle roles.
[*] [b]Vehicle renames:[/b] Traveller becomes [b]Chryslus Highwayman[/b], Highwayman becomes [b]Corvega APC[/b], and Prowler, Roadkill, Roadrunner, and Lightning become their Corvega-branded counterparts.
[*] [b]Upgrade terminology:[/b] renames compatible upgrades to Fallout-style parts including the [b]Chryslus Cherry Bomb[/b] sport engine, engine shroud, pintle mount, expanded cabin, wasteland tires, cowcatcher or ram bumper, and snow chains.
[*] [b]Combat and utility upgrades:[/b] further supported names include hood turret, welded armor kit, tail spoiler, armored tracks, gunner cupola, and trunk rack.
[*] [b]Supporting text:[/b] updates vehicle and upgrade labels, descriptions, research references, and connected interface wording consistently across the three required vehicle modules.
[*] [b]Mechanics scope:[/b] speed, fuel use, capacity, armor, weapons, construction, pathing, and upgrade effects remain those of Vanilla Vehicles Expanded.
[*] [b]Visual scope:[/b] Corvega currently ships no vehicle retextures; the source mods' models and textures remain visible under the new identities.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Vanilla Vehicles Expanded[/b] - Required for the vehicle framework and base models.
[*] [b]Vanilla Vehicles Expanded - Tier 3[/b] - Required for the supported high-tier vehicles.
[*] [b]Vanilla Vehicles Expanded - Upgrades[/b] - Required for the renamed upgrade set.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Corvega is a localization and branding module. It does not alter vehicle statistics or add a second upgrade framework.
[*] All three vehicle requirements are hard dependencies because the shipped patch set addresses content across the base, Tier 3, and Upgrades modules.
[*] Existing constructed vehicles may retain cached names in some menus until respawned or rebuilt.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
