[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Lucky 38[/h1]
[i]"Hospitality, house odds, and a complimentary Geiger counter."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Wasteland hospitality:[/b] converts supported guest, restaurant, spa, therapy, vending, and service content into Fallout-flavored businesses, facilities, food, and customer language.
[*] [b]Casino conversion:[/b] renames supported gambling content around the [b]Lucky 38[/b], [b]Vault 21[/b], and wasteland casino culture, including slot-machine and entertainment terminology.
[*] [b]Food and drink terminology:[/b] updates supported gastronomy, brewing, cocktails, sushi, vending, and ingredient content to Fallout products such as [b]atomic cocktails[/b], [b]homebrew sarsaparilla[/b], and [b]pungashine[/b].
[*] [b]Service automation:[/b] gives supported mech-waiter and hospitality automation content an appropriate RobCo-style identity when its source mod is active.
[*] [b]Spaceport conversion:[/b] renames supported spaceport shuttles and related services to [b]vertibirds[/b] and Fallout aviation terminology.
[*] [b]Visual replacements:[/b] includes a custom canned-ingredient texture and Fallout-themed replacements for the coffee workbench and espresso machine.
[*] [b]Renamed examples:[/b] Ambrandy becomes [b]pungashine[/b], soda becomes [b]homebrew sarsaparilla[/b], cocktails become atomic variants, and compatible shuttle labels become vertibirds.
[*] [b]Mechanics scope:[/b] guest needs, gambling odds, treatment rules, recipes, vending, and service logic remain those of the supported source mods.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] No DLC or external mod is a hard requirement.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Hospitality and its supported casino, spa, vending, therapy, gastronomy, and service add-ons[/b] - Optional; patches activate only for installed modules.
[*] [b]Supported food, brewing, cocktail, sushi, mech-waiter, and spaceport modules[/b] - Optional terminology and visual integration.
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3563825876][b]FIP - RobCo[/b][/url] - Recommended for a consistent robot-service presentation.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Lucky 38 is a broad compatibility, localization, and small visual-replacement module; it does not reproduce the mechanics of the mods it patches.
[*] Every integration is conditional, so this module can remain loaded without installing the full supported hospitality stack.
[*] Existing bills, menus, or generated businesses may retain cached wording until refreshed.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
