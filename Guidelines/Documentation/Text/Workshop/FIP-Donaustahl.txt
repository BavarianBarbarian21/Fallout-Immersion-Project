[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Donaustahl[/h1]
[i]"From Bavaria with love, and no patience for vanilla leftovers."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Fallout terminology cleanup:[/b] renames ultratech medicine to [b]Vault-Tec medicine[/b] and rewrites selected Vanilla Expanded content that still reads as medieval, space-fantasy, or generic RimWorld material.
[*] [b]Vanilla Events Expanded conversion:[/b] renames and rewrites ice age, global warming, psychic rain, long night, psychic bloom, space battle, shuttle crash, and cargo-pod events as [b]nuclear winter[/b], [b]scorched summer[/b], [b]temporal radstorm[/b], [b]ashen skies[/b], [b]mutant bloom[/b], [b]vertibird dogfight[/b], [b]vertibird crash[/b], and [b]cargo bot[/b] events.
[*] [b]Social and aspiration conversion:[/b] turns movie night into [b]holotape night[/b], snowmen into [b]snow brahmins[/b], binge parties into [b]camp blowouts[/b], and noble aspirations into joining the Enclave or attending a presidential speech.
[*] [b]Backstory cleanup:[/b] rewrites selected Vanilla Backstories Expanded space and royalty backgrounds as convoy, bunker, wasteland, and warlord histories, and removes the incompatible Oracle and Medieval Page backstories.
[*] [b]Books conversion:[/b] replaces Vanilla Books Expanded newspaper naming and descriptions with [b]Publick Occurrences[/b] wasteland issues.
[*] [b]Retextures and new mechanics:[/b] none in the current build. Donaustahl is a focused removal, naming, and description layer; it does not currently remove every vanilla faction or mechanoid by itself.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] No hard requirements - the base Vault-Tec medicine rename works on its own.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Vanilla Aspirations Expanded, Vanilla Backstories Expanded, Vanilla Events Expanded, Vanilla Social Interactions Expanded, Vanilla Traits Expanded, and Vanilla Books Expanded[/b] - Optional; each installed mod activates its matching cleanup.
[*] Other FIP modules - Optional; Donaustahl is intended as the final terminology-cleanup layer in a larger Fallout setup.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] The current repository does [b]not[/b] contain the broad vanilla-faction or RobCo mechanoid-removal patches claimed by the old description. This page lists only the cleanup that is actually shipped.
[*] Removing backstory definitions can affect pawn generation. A new game is recommended when using the Vanilla Backstories Expanded cleanup.
[*] This module is in open beta. Naming coverage and additional cleanup patches are still being expanded.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [/list]
