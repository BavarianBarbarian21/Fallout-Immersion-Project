[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - REPCONN[/h1]
[i]"Reach for the stars. Liability waivers are in the cockpit."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Hellion conversion:[/b] rewrites Odyssey's gravship presentation as a pre-war REPCONN [b]Hellion[/b] VTOL craft and updates the connected aerospace terminology.
[*] [b]Core renames:[/b] [b]gravship[/b] becomes [b]Hellion[/b], [b]gravcore[/b] becomes [b]reactor core[/b], [b]grav engine[/b] becomes [b]Hellion reactor[/b], thrusters become [b]VTOL[/b] hardware, and the pilot console becomes a [b]cockpit[/b].
[*] [b]Flight terminology:[/b] updates flight sickness, alerts, commands, construction text, quests, research, and interface language to match the REPCONN aircraft framing.
[*] [b]Vanilla Gravships Expanded support:[/b] conditionally converts its research, expertise, upgrades, components, and related labels into compatible Hellion and aerospace concepts.
[*] [b]Mechanics scope:[/b] construction, flight, fuel or power behavior, takeoff, landing, and upgrade rules remain those of Odyssey and the supported source mod.
[*] [b]Visual scope:[/b] REPCONN currently includes no in-game texture replacements; the visible craft still uses its source artwork.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Odyssey DLC[/b] - Required for gravship construction and flight systems.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Vanilla Gravships Expanded[/b] - Optional, with a conditional research and expertise terminology pass.
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3700612546][b]FIP - Poseidon[/b][/url] - Recommended for the surrounding energy and industrial terminology.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] REPCONN is a localization and setting-conversion module; it does not add a second flight framework.
[*] The Vanilla Gravships Expanded patch is inactive when that mod is not loaded.
[*] Existing craft or generated quests may retain cached names until rebuilt or regenerated.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
