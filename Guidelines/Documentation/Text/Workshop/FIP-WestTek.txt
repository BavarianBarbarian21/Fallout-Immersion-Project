[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - West Tek[/h1]
[i]"Humanity perfected. Side effects pending."[/i]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Fallout xenotypes:[/b] adds Atlantic, Cascadian, Nat'L, Nuevo Texico, Two California, Vault Dweller, Pure Human, Ghoul, first- and second-generation Super Mutant, Numen, Overgrown, Spore Carrier, S'Lanter, Skinwalker, and S'Nuffy lineages.
[*] [b]S.P.E.C.I.A.L. genes:[/b] turns Strength, Perception, Endurance, Charisma, Intelligence, Agility, and Luck into four gameplay tiers each, ranging from severe weaknesses to legendary advantages.
[*] [b]FEV research and progression:[/b] adds an ancient West Tek laboratory quest, the Forced Evolution start, FEV probes, samples, cultures, three dosage grades, staged research, and medical procedures that resolve into controlled or unstable mutations.
[*] [b]Fauna mutation mechanics:[/b] adds mutagens for unrefined, skinwalker, domestication, canine, feline, leporine, and procyonine routes; supports raccoon-to-S'Lanter progression and further Skinwalker or S'Nuffy outcomes; and includes hostile Floater results for invalid mutation routes.
[*] [b]Flora mutation mechanics:[/b] adds pollination and cultivation mutagens, Numen conversion from one symbiotic plant implant, Overgrown conversion at three implants, Spore Carrier infection, part-specific Numen ability genes, and a fertile-earth death effect for Spore Carriers.
[*] [b]Mutant creatures and gear:[/b] adds Centaurs, Floaters, Super Mutant Behemoths, tame Behemoths, Nightkin invisibility, Unity robes and hoods, three Super Mutant armor tiers, and the fire-hydrant club.
[*] [b]Visual replacements:[/b] includes custom bodies and corpses for Behemoths, Centaurs, Floaters, Nightkin, and Super Mutants; exclusive Super Mutant body and head textures; S'Lanter ears and tails; S.P.E.C.I.A.L. icons; ability icons; Unity apparel; and mutant sound effects.
[*] [b]Renamed vanilla content:[/b] replaces the vanilla Highmate xenotype with [b]S'Nuffy[/b] and patches compatible FCP ghoul, wendigo, Sanguophage, Vanilla Genetics Expanded, and Vanilla Races Expanded content.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] West Tek adds real transformation, gene, quest, and ability logic; it is not a localization-only xenotype pack.
[*] Mutation allowlists matter: unsupported targets are intentionally unchanged or produce the defined fallback instead of being silently converted.
[*] Removing the mod after its xenotypes, genes, hediffs, implants, or creatures exist in a save is not supported.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
