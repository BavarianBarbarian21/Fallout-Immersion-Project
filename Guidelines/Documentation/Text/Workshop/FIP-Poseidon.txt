[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Poseidon[/h1]
[i]"Powering tomorrow with yesterday's safety standards."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Fuel conversion:[/b] renames vanilla [b]chemfuel[/b] and its production chain to [b]oil[/b], including connected storage, generators, recipes, research, incidents, and interface text.
[*] [b]Gas conversion:[/b] conditionally rewrites Helixien Gas content as [b]propane[/b] infrastructure when that mod is loaded.
[*] [b]Fallout industry brands:[/b] maps supported power, medical, production, security, and factory content to names such as [b]Poseidon Energy[/b], [b]HELIOS Two[/b], [b]Corvega[/b], [b]Med-Tek[/b], and [b]Wattz Electronics[/b].
[*] [b]Broad compatibility layer:[/b] includes conditional terminology patches for Royalty, Anomaly, Helixien Gas, recycling, temperature control, Vanilla Chemfuel Expanded, and several Vanilla Furniture Expanded production and technology modules.
[*] [b]Industrial terminology:[/b] updates generators, conduits, batteries, fuel processing, manufacturing, medical equipment, spacer technology, nutrient production, defenses, and research labels where supported.
[*] [b]Mechanics scope:[/b] oil and propane retain the quantities, recipes, power output, storage rules, and behavior defined by vanilla or their source mods.
[*] [b]Visual scope:[/b] Poseidon currently ships no in-game texture replacements; it unifies names and descriptions across otherwise separate systems.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] No DLC or external mod is a hard requirement.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Helixien Gas, Vanilla Chemfuel Expanded, and supported Vanilla Furniture Expanded modules[/b] - Optional; each receives its own conditional Fallout terminology layer.
[*] Supported modules include Art, Factory, Medical, Power, Production, Security, Spacer, Nutrient, Recycling, and Temperature content where present.
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3688034883][b]FIP - REPCONN[/b][/url] - Recommended for a consistent aerospace and energy presentation.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Poseidon is a wide localization and compatibility module, not a new power-grid or fuel-simulation framework.
[*] Optional patches are isolated by source mod, so unsupported modules are neither required nor modified.
[*] Existing bills, stockpiles, or generated text may retain cached labels until recreated.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
