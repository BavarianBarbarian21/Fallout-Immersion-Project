[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - RobCo[/h1]
[i]"Automation for a brighter tomorrow - even if there's no one left to see it."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Fallout robot roster:[/b] adds Eyebots, Mister Handy and Mister Gutsy variants, Protectrons, Robobrains, Assaultrons, Sentry Bots, Securitrons, Liberty Prime, and further RobCo, General Atomics, and military machines to Biotech mechanitor play.
[*] [b]Robot production:[/b] provides custom gestator recipes, robot-specific ingredients and tiers, dedicated weapons, abilities, buildings, implants, apparel, and research paths instead of merely renaming vanilla mechs.
[*] [b]Mechanitor conversion:[/b] rethemes mechanitor progression, mech weight classes, gestators, subcores, control infrastructure, and related terminology around RobCo and General Atomics technology.
[*] [b]Signals, encounters, and bosses:[/b] adds robot boss groups, signal items, research-gated encounters, and Fallout-style salvage routes.
[*] [b]Platinum Chip questline:[/b] includes a multi-branch route with Courier and vault sites, plus an underground mechanic lair and its associated progression.
[*] [b]Additional characters:[/b] includes Synth and Think Tank xenotypes alongside the machine roster where the relevant systems are available.
[*] [b]Large audiovisual replacement:[/b] ships hundreds of custom robot, weapon, building, interface, and effect textures plus an extensive voice and sound library for the robot families.
[*] [b]Renamed content:[/b] vanilla mechanoid and mechanitor labels are converted to Fallout robotics terminology; individual machines retain distinct RobCo, General Atomics, military, or casino identities rather than sharing generic mech labels.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] RobCo contains new defs, recipes, quests, sites, abilities, encounters, textures, and audio; it is not a simple mech label patch.
[*] Content that depends on another DLC or supported mod is conditionally activated only when that source is loaded.
[*] Removing the mod after its robots, implants, quests, or sites exist in a save is not supported.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
