[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Greenway[/h1]
[i]"Faith grows where the old world finally cracks."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] Rebuilds [b]Ideology[/b] around Fallout-flavored beliefs, structures, and factional identity.
[*] Adds custom memes and religion defs such as the [b]New World Church[/b], [b]Old World Church[/b], and other wasteland belief systems.
[*] Reworks dryad and gauranlen-adjacent content into [b]Treefather[/b] and [b]Treeminder[/b] themed mechanics.
[*] Includes custom language data, textures, and nature patches while keeping [b]Odyssey[/b] optional.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Ideology DLC[/b] - Required (core gameplay systems)
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3665532846][b]FIP - H&H Tools[/b][/url] - Required (shared defs, name makers, and framework support)
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Odyssey DLC[/b] - Optional (extra compatibility headroom for broader finalized FIP integration)
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] This module is in open beta. Balance passes, naming cleanup, and compatibility refinements are still ongoing, so targeted feedback is useful.
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a complete modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but it is designed to complement FCP and other Fallout-friendly RimWorld mods. You can also find the project in the FCP Discord's FIP channel: [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]Join here[/url]
[/list]
