[h1]Fallout Immersion Project - Greenway[/h1]
[i]"A belief for every wasteland - and a wasteland for every belief."[/i]

[url=https://ko-fi.com/misterhouse][img]https://i.imgur.com/YUpX4Up.png[/img][/url]

[b][Open Beta][/b] This mod is under active development - updates and patches may drop anytime.
Your feedback directly shapes development!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Fallout ideologies:[/b] adds four government memes and roughly twenty further custom memes covering wasteland societies, factions, technology, survival, mutation, and faith.
[*] [b]Religious identities:[/b] supplies Fallout-themed religious groups, terminology, symbols, and naming so generated ideoligions fit the setting.
[*] [b]Mutant policy mechanic:[/b] adds a dedicated issue with precepts and thought workers that make a colony's acceptance or rejection of mutants affect beliefs and mood.
[*] [b]Treefather conversion:[/b] renames and rewrites the Gauranlen system around the Treefather and Treeminders, including connected ceremonies, bonds, sprouts, and dryad terminology.
[*] [b]New Pungaling dryad:[/b] adds a custom dryad form to the Treefather ecosystem instead of only relabeling an existing pawn.
[*] [b]Plants, food, and chems:[/b] integrates punga-themed content, brahmin shrooms, and Fallout drug terminology; supported FCP chem recipes use psychoid leaves where appropriate.
[*] [b]Visual replacements:[/b] includes a Treefather tree retexture, custom Pungaling and Treeminder art, brahmin-shroom textures, and icons for the new memes.
[*] [b]Renamed vanilla content:[/b] Gauranlen trees, dryads, tree connections, and related Ideology language become Treefather and Treeminder concepts throughout the supported content.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Ideology DLC[/b] - Required for memes, precepts, rituals, and the Treefather system.
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3665532846][b]FIP - H&H Tools[/b][/url] - Required for shared cultures, factions, and terminology.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3592202407][b]FIP - West Tek[/b][/url] - Recommended for the xenotypes and mutants evaluated by the mutant-policy precepts.
[*] [b]FCP content modules[/b] - Optional recipes and terminology are patched only when their source mods are loaded.
[/list]

[h2]Development Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Greenway contains new memes, precepts, thought logic, and a new dryad; it is more than an Ideology vocabulary pass.
[*] Conditional patches do not make their source mods hard requirements.
[*] Existing ideoligions may not automatically gain newly added memes or precepts; starting a new belief system gives the cleanest result.
[*] [url=https://github.com/BavarianBarbarian21/Fallout-Immersion-Project]Source code and development repository[/url]
[*] This mod is part of the Fallout Immersion Project (FIP) - a modular overhaul inspired by Fallout's lore. FIP is not affiliated with the Fallout Collaboration Project (FCP), but is designed to complement it. [url=https://discordapp.com/channels/644497305650593793/1414315188483657869]FIP channel in the FCP Discord[/url]
[/list]
