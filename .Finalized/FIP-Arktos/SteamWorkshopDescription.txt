[h1]FIP - Arktos[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

Arktos rebuilds RimWorld's biome and world generation from the ground up, replacing its generic landscapes with the iconic environments of the Fallout universe. From sun-baked Mojave desert canyons to ancient redwood forests and irradiated wasteland craters, every world you generate will feel distinctly post-apocalyptic.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]23 custom Fallout biomes[/b] replacing vanilla RimWorld biomes — including Mojave, Canyon, Mesa, Wasteland, Malpais, Cranberry Bog, Brahmin Woods, Floodplains, Aqueduct, Redwood, Sequoia, Divide, Marina, Ruin, Crater, and more
[*] [b]Custom world features[/b] matching the geographic variety of the Fallout universe: arctic ice sheets and glacial plains, desert canyons and valleys, coastal marinas, and dense forest preserves
[*] [b]Fallout-themed world landmarks[/b] (requires Odyssey): suburbs, stores, gas stations, construction sites, harbors, junkyards, uplink stations, stockpile bunkers, launch sites, and military garrisons
[*] [b]Industrial vent landmarks[/b]: Ancient Smoke Vents, Tox Vents, and Heat Vents scattered across the world map
[*] [b]Fire Ant colony system[/b]: Fire Ant Queens, Soldiers, Workers, Drones, Larvae, and Cocoons with insect jelly production — a faithful recreation of the New Vegas Fire Ant threat
[*] [b]Infested settlement events[/b]: Abandoned settlements overrun by regular insects or Fire Ant colonies
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]FIP - H&H Tools[/b] (required) — core FIP shared resource layer
[*] [b]RimWorld: Odyssey[/b] — landmark defs and quest patches activate automatically when Odyssey is loaded; the mod works without it
[*] [b]FCP - Plants Pack[/b] (Fallout Community Project) — biome plant tables include FCP plants when present
[*] [b]FCP - Animals Pack[/b] (Fallout Community Project) — biome creature tables include FCP animals when present
[*] [b]Vanilla Plants Expanded[/b] — plant table integration patch included
[*] [b]Vanilla Plants Expanded: More Plants[/b] — plant table integration patch included

[*] [b]Vanilla Animals Expanded: Royalty[/b] — animal table integration patch included
[*] [b]Vanilla Animals Expanded: Wasteland[/b] — animal table integration patch included
