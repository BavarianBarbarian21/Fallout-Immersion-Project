[h1]FIP - H&H Tools[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

H&H Tools is the shared foundation that all other FIP mods build on. It provides the common definitions, cultural templates, faction scaffolding, and centralized naming rule sets that give every FIP mod a consistent Fallout identity. Install it first; the rest of the project depends on it.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Regional culture definitions[/b] — distinct post-apocalyptic cultures for the American Southwest and West Coast: Arizona, California, Cascadia, and Texico, each with their own naming conventions and societal traits
[*] [b]Ghoul and Mutant faction templates[/b] — shared base faction definitions for irradiated and FEV-mutated population groups, ready to be extended by other FIP mods
[*] [b]Slanter and Numen faction templates[/b] — additional regional population archetypes used across the FIP ecosystem
[*] [b]Pawn kind definitions[/b] — base and apparel-variant pawn kinds that reflect regional and factional identities across the Fallout world
[*] [b]Centralized naming rule sets[/b] — comprehensive name makers for characters, settlements, and factions, ensuring every generated name fits the Fallout setting
[*] [b]Settlement and place name tables[/b] — world location naming rules covering every culture and region defined in the mod
[*] [b]Faction patches[/b] — retheming patches for a wide range of vanilla and DLC factions: Outlander Refugees, Pilgrims, Sanguophages, Salvagers, Horax Cultists, Traders Guilds, Research Expeditions, Ghouls, Entities, Ancients, Empire, Insects, and player-controlled tribes and colonies
[/list]
