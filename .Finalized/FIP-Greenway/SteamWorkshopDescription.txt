[h1]FIP - Greenway[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

Greenway rebuilds RimWorld's Ideology, dryad, and Gauranlen tree systems through a Fallout lens. It replaces generic ideological memes with belief systems rooted in post-apocalyptic American culture — from Old World religion and desert ranger codes of honor to Brahmin baron economics and underground railroad networks. The Gauranlen symbiosis system is reimagined around the Punga plant and the Pungaling Treeminder caste.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Pungaling Treeminder caste[/b] — a specialized Ideology caste with a symbiotic bond to Punga trees, replacing the vanilla Gauranlen system; Treeminders move slowly but excel at tending their unique Punga groves for food and materials
[*] [b]17+ custom ideological memes[/b] covering the full spectrum of Fallout factions and philosophies:
  [list]
  [*] [b]Old World Church / New World Church[/b] — competing religious visions for the post-apocalypse
  [*] [b]Desert Rangers[/b] — abolitionist law enforcers committed to justice in the wastes
  [*] [b]Railroad[/b] — underground liberation network dedicated to freeing the oppressed
  [*] [b]Greaser[/b] — power armor rebels living by the creed "Every Man a King"
  [*] [b]Brahmin Barons[/b] — livestock-controlling economic elite who dominate wasteland trade
  [*] [b]Working Camps / Indentured Servitude / Mutant Slaves[/b] — labor and slavery philosophies
  [*] [b]Vice City[/b] — settlements built on gambling and illicit economies
  [*] [b]Purity Law[/b] — Nukashine brewing culture with strict regulations
  [*] [b]Chem Trade / Jet Road[/b] — chem-trafficking ideologies
  [*] [b]Mutant Citizenship vs. Mutant Purge[/b] — opposing stances on non-human populations
  [*] [b]Cannibalist Families / Survivalist Lifestyle / Cloud Residue[/b] — fringe survival philosophies
  [*] [b]Follower Support / Enclave Scientists in Exile / Book Burning[/b] — knowledge and solidarity memes
  [/list]
[*] [b]Odyssey compatibility[/b] — Odyssey-specific hooks are separated into optional integration that activates only when Odyssey is loaded
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]RimWorld: Ideology[/b] (required) — all content in this mod depends on the Ideology DLC
[*] [b]FIP - H&H Tools[/b] (required) — core FIP shared resource layer
[*] [b]RimWorld: Odyssey[/b] — optional; Odyssey-specific Treeminder and Treefather content activates automatically when Odyssey is present
[/list]
