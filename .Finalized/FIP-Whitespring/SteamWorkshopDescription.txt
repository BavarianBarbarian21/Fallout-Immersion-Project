[h1]FIP - Whitespring[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

Whitespring transforms Royalty's Empire faction and honor system into the Whitespring Enclave — a militaristic survivor organization claiming descent from Appalachia's pre-war scientific establishment. They study cryptid phenomena, pursue old-world order, and reward loyalty with political capital. Every mechanical layer of Royalty is rewritten to fit this vision, from rank titles to shuttle craft.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Whitespring Enclave faction[/b] replaces the Empire — "Militant enclave of survivors claiming to represent the Appalachian scientific division," with its own leadership structure, pawn titles, and cryptid research agenda
[*] [b]President[/b] replaces the Empire's ruler title — the elected/installed head of the Whitespring Enclave
[*] [b]Ranks[/b] replace Titles — a military hierarchy rather than noble peerage
[*] [b]Intel[/b] replaces Royal Favor — the political currency of the Enclave; earned through loyalty and field operations
[*] [b]Votes[/b] replace Honor — the democratic legitimacy that underpins Enclave authority
[*] [b]Vertibirds[/b] replace Shuttles — Enclave VTOL aircraft used for troop transport and VIP extraction
[*] [b]Whitespring Agents[/b] — the rank-and-file pawn kind of the Enclave, with Enclave-themed apparel replacing Empire outfits
[*] [b]Cryptid occultism integration[/b] — Mothman Totem replaces the Anima Tree, Mothman Eggs replace Anima Grass, and Cryptic Revelations replace psycast terminology, mirroring FIP-Hubris
[*] [b]Full apparel overhaul[/b] — Empire garments replaced with Enclave-themed uniforms and gear
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]RimWorld: Royalty[/b] (required) — all content in this mod depends on the Royalty DLC
[*] [b]FCP - Enclave Pack[/b] (Fallout Community Project) — Enclave apparel integration patch activates when this pack is loaded
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Empire[/b] — compatibility patch included; VFE Empire content is integrated into the Whitespring Enclave aesthetic when this mod is loaded
[*] [b]Vanilla Factions Expanded - Deserters[/b] — compatibility patch included; Deserter content interacts correctly with the Whitespring faction structure
[*] [b]FIP - Donaustahl[/b] — optional but recommended; load Whitespring before Donaustahl
[/list]
