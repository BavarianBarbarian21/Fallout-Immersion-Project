[h1]FIP - Hubris[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

Hubris reimagines Royalty's anima tree and psycast systems as cryptid occultism for a Fallout playthrough. The supernatural fringe of the Fallout universe — Mothman sightings, cryptid cults, mysterious psychic phenomena — comes to life as a full replacement for RimWorld's psylink mechanics. Every tooltip, UI label, and in-game term is rewritten to fit the lore.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Psycast system fully reskinned as Cryptid Occultism[/b] — every vanilla Royalty psycast term is replaced with Fallout-appropriate cryptid terminology
[*] [b]Mothman Totem[/b] replaces the Anima Tree — the eerie focus point for occultist meditation and cryptid connection rituals
[*] [b]Mothman Eggs[/b] replace Anima Grass — scattered around the Mothman Totem, harvested and used in cryptid rituals
[*] [b]Occultists[/b] replace Psycasters — pawns who channel cryptid insight rather than psyfocus
[*] [b]Cryptid Insight[/b] replaces Psylink — the mysterious bond between a pawn and the cryptid entities they commune with
[*] [b]Cryptid Focus[/b] replaces Psyfocus — the meditative energy Occultists build through their rituals
[*] [b]Cryptic Revelations[/b] replace Neurotrainers — items that unlock new levels of cryptid connection
[*] [b]Cryptid Essence[/b] replaces psycast consumables — rare materials tied to the cryptid world
[*] [b]Expanded meditation focus types[/b] — Occultists can meditate at Enclave posts, cryptic shrines, totems, blank walls, and graves in addition to standard focus objects
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]RimWorld: Royalty[/b] (required) — all content in this mod depends on the Royalty DLC
[*] [b]FIP - Donaustahl[/b] — optional but recommended; load Hubris after Donaustahl for a fully immersive faction setup
[/list]
