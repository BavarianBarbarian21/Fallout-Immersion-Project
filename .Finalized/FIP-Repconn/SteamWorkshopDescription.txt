[h1]FIP - Repconn[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

Repconn rebuilds Odyssey's gravship and aerospace systems around the Fallout universe's aerospace heritage. REPCONN Aerospace was the company behind some of the most ambitious pre-war rocketry programs, and their legacy lives on in the rusting hulks and salvaged spacecraft of the wasteland. Every gravship becomes a Hellion — a cobbled-together vessel born from pre-war blueprints and post-war ingenuity.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Hellion spacecraft[/b] replaces vanilla gravships — all gravship terminology is rewritten to reflect REPCONN's pre-war aerospace branding
[*] [b]Hellion Reactor[/b] replaces the Grav Engine — the volatile power core keeping your vessel airborne
[*] [b]Hellion Deck Plating[/b] replaces Substructure — the reinforced flooring of your Hellion hull
[*] [b]Hellion Electromagnetic Field[/b] replaces the Anima Field — the defensive energy envelope surrounding the vessel
[*] [b]VTOL thrusters, fuel systems, and pilot consoles[/b] — aerospace infrastructure components for constructing and operating Hellions
[*] [b]Mooring masts[/b] — ground-based docking structures for landing and servicing Hellion spacecraft
[*] [b]Orbital platforms and asteroid mining operations[/b] — late-game aerospace content extending the Hellion playstyle into space
[*] [b]Vertibird transport variants[/b] — shuttle and troop transport configurations styled after Fallout's iconic VTOL aircraft
[*] [b]ECM suite and heavy weapons systems[/b] — combat-focused Hellion equipment
[*] [b]Robot Command Hub[/b] replaces the Mechanoid Hive — the control center for automated robot formations aboard or near your vessel
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]RimWorld: Odyssey[/b] (required) — all content in this mod depends on the Odyssey DLC
[*] [b]Vanilla Gravship Expanded[/b] — compatibility patch included; all Vanilla Gravship Expanded content is renamed and integrated into the Hellion aesthetic when this mod is loaded
[/list]
