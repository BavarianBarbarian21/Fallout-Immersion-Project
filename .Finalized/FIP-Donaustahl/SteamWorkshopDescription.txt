[h1]FIP - Donaustahl[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

Donaustahl is the optional finishing touch for a fully immersive Fallout playthrough. Once you have the other FIP mods in place and want to cut every last remnant of vanilla RimWorld identity from your game, this is the mod for you. It removes all non-Fallout factions from world generation, ensuring that every faction you encounter is part of the Fallout setting.

[b]⚠ Warning:[/b] If you still want vanilla mechanoids or vanilla RimWorld factions in your game, do [b]not[/b] install this mod. Use the other FIP mods on their own instead.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Vanilla faction removal[/b] — removes all non-Fallout-flavored factions from world creation, including tribal, outlander, and pirate faction templates that do not fit the Fallout setting
[*] [b]Full immersion patch[/b] — works as a global override so that only FIP-defined factions and cultures appear in the world
[*] [b]FIP-RobCo integration[/b] — includes a dedicated patch to ensure the RobCo robot faction system is correctly wired in when Donaustahl is active
[*] [b]No required DLCs[/b] — works as a standalone addition on top of any combination of other FIP mods
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] No non-FIP mods are required. Donaustahl is intentionally dependency-free so it can sit on top of any FIP mod stack without pulling in additional requirements.
[*] Designed to be loaded [b]after[/b] all other FIP mods that define Fallout factions (e.g. FIP-RobCo), so those factions are already registered before Donaustahl removes the vanilla defaults.
[/list]
