[h1]FIP - WestTek[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

WestTek brings the biological diversity of the Fallout universe to your colony. West Tek Research Incorporated pioneered the FEV virus and power armor programs that shaped post-war humanity — and the consequences are everywhere. This mod adds the full spectrum of Fallout's human variants as playable xenotypes, from sheltered Vault Dwellers to irradiated Ghouls and hulking Super Mutants, each built from a custom gene system grounded in SPECIAL stats.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]11 Fallout xenotypes[/b] covering every major human variant in the setting:
  [list]
  [*] [b]Vault Dweller[/b] — sheltered, educated, and naive to the surface; disciplined but unprepared for the wasteland
  [*] [b]Super Mutant[/b] — FEV-mutated humans; massive, powerful, green-skinned, and nearly indestructible
  [*] [b]Ghoul[/b] — radiation-twisted remnants of the old world; deathless, highly radiation-resistant, pale and desiccated
  [*] [b]Numen[/b] — spiritually mutated strain with unique metaphysical characteristics
  [*] [b]Regional human variants:[/b] SLanter, Cascadia, Nuevo Texico, NatL (natural/tribal), Atlantic, and Two California — each adapted to their post-war environment
  [*] [b]Pure Human[/b] — unmodified baseline humans for those who survived without significant mutation
  [/list]
[*] [b]SPECIAL stat gene system[/b] — genes that directly modify Strength, Perception, Endurance, Charisma, Intelligence, Agility, and Luck, allowing fine-grained control over a pawn's Fallout-style stat profile
[*] [b]Ability genes[/b] — weapon skill aptitudes and task specializations as heritable traits
[*] [b]Health genes[/b] — radiation resistance, toxin resistance, temperature tolerance, and longevity genes reflecting each xenotype's survival adaptations
[*] [b]Mutation and visual genes[/b] — skin color variants, body type differences, and hair options tied to xenotype identity
[*] [b]Sanguophage integration[/b] — compatibility patch ensuring WestTek xenotypes interact correctly with the Sanguophage system
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]RimWorld: Biotech[/b] (required) — all content in this mod depends on the Biotech DLC
[*] [b]FIP - H&H Tools[/b] (required) — core FIP shared resource layer providing culture and faction scaffolding
[*] [b]FCP - Ghouls Pack[/b] (Fallout Community Project) — Ghoul xenotype integration patch activates when this pack is loaded; load WestTek after FCP Ghouls
[*] [b]FIP - Donaustahl[/b] — optional but recommended; load WestTek before Donaustahl
[/list]
