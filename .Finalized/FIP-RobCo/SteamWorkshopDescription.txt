[h1]FIP - RobCo[/h1]

Part of the [b]Fallout Immersion Project (FIP)[/b] — a suite of mods that transforms RimWorld into a faithful Fallout experience.

RobCo completely reimagines Biotech's mechanoid system as the robots of the Fallout universe. Every vanilla mech is replaced with an iconic RobCo Industries or Chinese military machine, from the humble Protectron patrolling a ruined office block to the towering Liberty Prime marching to war. Custom sounds, models, and AI behaviors bring each robot to life in a way that feels true to the source material.

[hr]

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]7 iconic base robot types[/b] — Protectron, Assaultron, Eyebot, Mr. Handy, Robobrain, Cyberdog, and Sentry Bot, each with unique stats and behaviors
[*] [b]15+ specialized robot variants[/b], including:
  [list]
  [*] [b]Assaultron Sheepsquatch[/b] — rare cryptid-themed combat variant
  [*] [b]Eyebot Duraframe[/b] — reinforced scouting platform
  [*] [b]Mr. Gutsy[/b] — military combat android, [b]Mr. Nanny[/b] — domestic service android, [b]Collectron[/b] — resource gathering unit
  [*] [b]Constructron[/b] — automated builder, [b]Factotron[/b] — industrial factory unit, [b]Utilitron[/b] — general-purpose utility robot
  [*] [b]Sentry Bot variants:[/b] Annihilator, Pestcontrol, Siegebreaker, and Deathbot
  [*] [b]Hydra variants:[/b] Eye of Hydra and Gutsy Hydra
  [/list]
[*] [b]House faction robots[/b] — Liberty Prime (colossal combat platform) and Securitron (House security force)
[*] [b]Chinese military robots[/b] — Liberator combat unit and War Machine heavy platform
[*] [b]Custom audio for every robot type[/b] — unique sound sets sourced from Fallout audio design
[*] [b]Full robot crafting system[/b] — recipes, components, bodies, body parts, and AI subcores for building and upgrading robots
[*] [b]Complete Biotech retheming[/b] — all mechanoid terminology replaced:
  [list]
  [*] "Mechanoid" → "Robot" | "Mech" → "Robot"
  [*] "Mechanitor" → "Overseer"
  [*] "Mech Workshop" → "Robot Workshop"
  [*] "Mech Cluster" → "Robot Formation"
  [/list]
[*] [b]Faction integration[/b] — robot factions connect to the broader FIP faction ecosystem
[/list]

[hr]

[h2]Compatibility:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]RimWorld: Biotech[/b] (required) — all content in this mod depends on the Biotech DLC
[*] [b]FIP - Donaustahl[/b] — optional but recommended; load RobCo before Donaustahl so robot factions are registered before vanilla factions are removed
[/list]
